Hello and welcome to my online portfolio and resume! I'm a senior journalism major at the University of Maryland, focusing in sports broadcasting. Growing up a ballerina from the Jersey Shore, I had an odd way of finding my path in sports journalism. After undergoing knee surgery to repair an injury when I was 16, I found myself watching all of my dad's favorite sports shows while I healed, and fell in love with the world of sports. I then began to teach myself everything I could learn about sports, and the more I took in sports media, I realized I wanted to be a part of it. I was thrilled to be accepted to my first choice school, the Philip Merrill College of Journalism at the University of Maryland, and I've spent the last four years riding a nonstop train of experience in the field. I'm at my happiest when I'm on the sidelines with a video camera, anchoring live shows, and sitting down with athletes to tell the important stories about who they are both on and off the field. I will be graduating in May 2022, and am eagerly seeking opportunities as a full-time TV sports reporter/MMJ.
